Who We Are

Nigeria's Circular Economy
Champion

360° Waste Solutions

TerraForgeEco is a forward-thinking waste management and environmental services company dedicated to transforming waste into value across Nigeria. Established to address the growing challenges of urban waste, we integrate recycling, upcycling, composting, renewable energy conversion, product manufacturing, and eco-cleaning solutions into a circular economy model.

We operate at the intersection of innovation, sustainability, and community impact — creating jobs, protecting the environment, and driving sustainable growth across Adamawa State and beyond.

Fully incorporated under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria, with compliance to environmental, health, and business regulations.

🎯 Our Vision

To be Nigeria's leading catalyst for a clean, sustainable, and circular economy where waste is redefined as a resource.

🚀 Our Mission

To deliver innovative, eco-friendly waste management and cleaning solutions that empower communities and generate lasting economic opportunities.

What We Offer

Our Core Services

From waste collection to end-product development, our integrated service model creates a complete circular economy solution.

Service 01

Waste Recycling & Upcycling

Plastic, e-waste, textile, agricultural, and construction debris recycling. We convert waste streams into reusable consumer and industrial products, reducing landfill burden dramatically.

Service 02

Composting & Energy Solutions

Organic waste-to-compost production and waste-to-energy innovations. We turn food and agricultural waste into soil amendments for farmers and clean energy alternatives.

Laundry & Car Wash Services
